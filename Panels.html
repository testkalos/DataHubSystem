Panels Description

The DHuS provides the Administrator a serires of Panels to fulfil every service. We report here how to access them using the GUI. The list of panels is provided here below:

1. Search Panel;

2. Upload Panel;

3. Profile Panel;

4. Cart Panel;

5. Management Panel (Users, Collections, System, Eviction);

6. Odata synchronizer Panel;

Once the administrator has logged in, the panels are accessible clicking on the user icon on the upper right side of the page.
