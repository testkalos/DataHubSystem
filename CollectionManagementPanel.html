Collection Management Panel

Products are gathered into collections. Collections management consists of:

1.Creating or deleting collections;

2.Adding a sub collection or a collection parent.

The Collection management panel also lists a set of products to be attached to the collection. The selection of collections is possible by browsing thecollection hierarchy on the left. To access the collection management panel, the Administrator has to click on the collections link, sited in the upper left side of the management panel. The collection management panel here below will open. It contains the list of collections on the left and the list of archived products on the right.
