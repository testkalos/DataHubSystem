FOSS-4 GIS EUROPE

FOSS4G Europe 2017 will be held in Marne-la-Vallée on July 18th-22nd 2017. The third edition of the conference is organized at École Nationale des Sciences Géographiques (ENSG). Our event aims at bringing Open Source GIS users and developers together and fostering closer interactions amongst the european geospatial communities.
