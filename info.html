Info

The Sentinels Scientific Data Hub is a web based system designed to provide EO data users with distributed mirror archives and bulk dissemination capabilities for the Sentinels products. Data Hub System developed by a Serco and GAEL Systems consortium under a contract with the European Space Agency in the frame of the Copernicus Programme.
